# The Autonomous Network: Powered by Agentic AI

Today's networks have surpassed human scale. The sheer complexity and velocity of data generated by cloud, edge computing, and private 5G have made traditional network management obsolete. Constant firefighting and reactive problem-solving are no longer sustainable. It's time for the network to manage itself.

This is the future of network observability, and it's powered by Agentic AI. At its core, Agentic AI transforms your network from a passive system that simply reports problems into an autonomous entity that can reason, decide, and act. It's an intelligence layer that understands your business goals and works proactively to achieve them—without waiting for human command.

## Cirries DART: Where Agentic AI Comes to Life

The Cirries DART platform is built on the principles of Agentic AI, creating a truly intelligent observability solution that delivers a new level of performance and reliability.

### Goal-Oriented Performance, Not Static Rules

DART doesn't just monitor metrics; it understands intent. Whether your goal is maintaining stringent SLA compliance, ensuring ultra-low latency for a critical application, or guaranteeing bandwidth for a priority service, our Agentic AI works continuously to meet those objectives. It dynamically adjusts routing, prioritizes traffic, and reallocates resources in real time, moving beyond the limitations of rigid, pre-defined rules.

### From Diagnosis to Resolution, Autonomously

When a performance issue arises, legacy systems send an alert. DART initiates a solution. Our AI agents go beyond surface-level symptoms to perform an immediate and autonomous root cause analysis, digging into packet-level data to find the *why* behind the problem. It then takes independent, self-healing actions—rerouting traffic, isolating a compromised node, or restarting a service—to resolve the issue, often before human operators are even aware of it.

### Predictive Assurance to Prevent Outages

The ultimate goal is to solve problems before they impact your business. Using advanced predictive models, DART's Agentic AI forecasts future network states to identify potential outages and bottlenecks. This allows the network to take preemptive action, ensuring seamless service continuity and transforming your operations from reactive to predictive.
